Community Board

Your central hub for server features — press Alt+B in-game to open it.

The Community Board is a custom in-game panel that gives access to all of Kairos's extended features from one place. Open it at any time by pressing Alt+B. The left-hand menu lists every available section — click any entry to navigate to it.

Buffer

The Buffer lets you build a personal buff scheme — a saved list of buffs that can be applied to your character with a single click. No NPC hunting required once your scheme is set up.

Access

Open the Community Board with Alt+B, then select Buffer from the left menu.

Building Your Scheme

The Buffer panel shows groups of buff icons arranged in columns. Each group represents a category of buffs (combat, support, etc.). To add a buff to your scheme:

  1. Find the buff icon you want inside one of the columns.
  2. Click the + button next to it.
  3. The buff is now part of your saved scheme.

Your scheme is saved per character profile and persists between sessions — you only need to set it up once.

Applying and Clearing

Button What it does
Apply Scheme Applies all buffs currently saved in your scheme to your character.
Clean Scheme Removes all buffs from your saved scheme. Does not remove active buffs on your character.

Buffer Level & Progression

The Buffer has its own experience bar. A higher Buffer Level unlocks additional buff slots in your scheme, giving you access to more buffs at once.

EXP Gain L-Coin Cost
+1,000 EXP 1,000 L-Coin
+5,000 EXP 5,000 L-Coin
+10,000 EXP 10,000 L-Coin
Requirement

Buffer leveling is locked until you have completed approximately 150 Rebirths. The L-Coin purchase buttons will not be available before that threshold is reached.

L-Coin

L-Coin is the currency used to purchase Buffer EXP. It drops from:

  • Regular mobs throughout the world
  • Raid and world bosses
  • Seasonal and special in-game events
Tip

Focus on grinding zones where L-Coin drop rates are highest. Boss kills tend to give larger L-Coin drops than regular mobs.

Faction War

Faction War is a simple server-wide competition between two factions. Choose a side, contribute to help it grow, and earn powerful rewards as your faction levels up.

The Two Factions

There are two factions competing at all times:

FactionDescription
Order of EinhasadThe faction of light — join to fight under Einhasad's banner.
Cult of ShilenThe faction of darkness — join to serve under Shilen's power.
Keep the Balance

Only the leading faction receives the buff. To keep the competition fair and both sides rewarded, try to join the faction with fewer members when you first choose a side. Check the member count on the panel before joining.

How It Works

  1. Open the Community Board (Alt+B) and select Faction War.
  2. Click Join Einhasad or Join Shilen to pick your faction.
  3. Contribute Gold Bars using the Contribute EXP button to increase your faction's experience.
  4. As the faction's level rises, members receive new items and rewards.
  5. The faction currently in the lead earns the Faction Leader passive buff for all its members.

Faction Leader Buff

When your faction is leading, every member automatically receives the Faction Leader passive skill. The buff scales with faction level — the stats below are for Level 1:

StatBonus (Lv 1)
PvE Damage+15%
Drop Rate+10%
Spoil Rate+10%
Adena Amount+15%
Max HP+1,000
Speed+35

Rewards & Rankings

Use the buttons at the bottom of the panel to track progress and claim rewards:

ButtonAction
Contribute EXPSpend Gold Bars to add EXP to your faction's total.
View RewardsSee the items unlocked at each faction level.
View RankingsCheck top contributors across both factions.
Tip

New items are unlocked progressively as the faction levels up — check View Rewards regularly to see what's coming next and plan your Gold Bar contributions accordingly.

MultiSkill

MultiSkill lets you learn passive and active skills from any retail class in the game, as well as exclusive Custom Skills unique to Kairos. Skills are organised by class and grouped into progression tiers — the higher the tier, the stronger the skill and the rarer the material required.

Cost Rule

First time learning a skill: Adena + Class Stone + SP
Upgrading an existing skill: Adena + SP only — no stone needed.

Retail Classes

All retail classes are available and grouped by their class-change tier. Each tier requires a specific Class Stone to unlock the first level of any skill in that group. Stones drop from monsters in the corresponding farming zones.

Tier Class Change Level Requirement Stone Required
Tier 1 Starting Classes — Class Stone I
Tier 2 1st Class Change Level 20 Class Stone II
Tier 3 2nd Class Change Level 40 Class Stone III
Tier 4 3rd Class Change Level 76 Class Stone IV
Example

Want to learn the Orc Shaman Weapon Mastery skill? It's a starting-class skill — grab a Class Stone I from a low-level farming zone, open MultiSkill, find the Orc class, and learn it. Subsequent level upgrades cost Adena + SP only. See Getting Started — Mana Fix for a walkthrough.

Custom Skills

At the bottom of the MultiSkill panel sits the Custom Skills section — a set of exclusive skills available only on Kairos. These follow the same learning pattern as retail skills but use a rarer material obtained in higher-level zones.

Property Details
Stone Required Class Stone V — drops in higher-level farming zones
First Unlock Adena + Class Stone V + SP
Level Upgrades Adena + SP only (no additional stone)
Skill Type All custom skills are passive
Enchanting Supported — use the standard Lineage 2 skill enchantment system
Hint

All Custom Skills are passive — they work automatically once learned with no activation needed. They can also be enchanted using the standard L2 skill enchantment system, making them significantly stronger at higher enchant levels.
