Community Board

Your central hub for server features — press Alt+B in-game to open it.

The Community Board is a custom in-game panel that gives access to all of Kairos's extended features from one place. Open it at any time by pressing Alt+B. The left-hand menu lists every available section — click any entry to navigate to it.

Buffer

The Buffer lets you build a personal buff scheme — a saved list of buffs that can be applied to your character with a single click. No NPC hunting required once your scheme is set up.

Access

Open the Community Board with Alt+B, then select Buffer from the left menu.

Building Your Scheme

The Buffer panel shows groups of buff icons arranged in columns. Each group represents a category of buffs (combat, support, etc.). To add a buff to your scheme:

  1. Find the buff icon you want inside one of the columns.
  2. Click the + button next to it.
  3. The buff is now part of your saved scheme.

Your scheme is saved per character profile and persists between sessions — you only need to set it up once.

Applying and Clearing

Button What it does
Apply Scheme Applies all buffs currently saved in your scheme to your character.
Clean Scheme Removes all buffs from your saved scheme. Does not remove active buffs on your character.

Buffer Level & Progression

The Buffer has its own experience bar. A higher Buffer Level unlocks additional buff slots in your scheme, giving you access to more buffs at once.

EXP Gain L-Coin Cost
+1,000 EXP 1,000 L-Coin
+5,000 EXP 5,000 L-Coin
+10,000 EXP 10,000 L-Coin
Requirement

Buffer leveling is locked until you have completed approximately 150 Rebirths. The L-Coin purchase buttons will not be available before that threshold is reached.

L-Coin

L-Coin is the currency used to purchase Buffer EXP. It drops from:

  • Regular mobs throughout the world
  • Raid and world bosses
  • Seasonal and special in-game events
Tip

Focus on grinding zones where L-Coin drop rates are highest. Boss kills tend to give larger L-Coin drops than regular mobs.
